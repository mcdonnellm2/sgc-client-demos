The Story

Smoke Is Our Secret

We didn't set out to build a restaurant. We set out to make the best sub anyone in Central Kentucky had ever tasted.

Every meat is smoked in-house. Every sauce is made from scratch. Every sub is built to order and handed to you hot. That's it. That's the whole thing.

We only open three days a week because that's how long it takes to do it right. Thursdays, Fridays, and Saturdays in Harrodsburg — and that's where you'll find us.
